Double Tax Avoidance Agreement (DTAA)
India has DTAA with 90+ countries. NRIs can claim the lower of India's tax rate or DTAA rate on eligible income. To claim DTAA benefit, the NRI must furnish Tax Residency Certificate (TRC) + Form 10F.
